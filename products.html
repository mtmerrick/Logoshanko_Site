Industry Index

Industry Index is an extension for various Web Browsers, that integrates with Indeed.

This extension provides you with valuable additional information regarding actual pay and standard wages, presented front and center to help you make smart job-hunting decisions.

Current functionality includes Living Wage calculations by state, State and Local minimum wages, and approximate takehomes after standard withholdings.

This is an active project! Expect additional functionality and broader avaliability in the future!
